Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 1, 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide useful information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Authentication cookies: Remember your login status
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Performance cookies: Help us improve website performance

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

  • Session cookies for maintaining login state
  • CSRF tokens for security
  • Load balancing cookies

Analytics Cookies

  • Video view tracking
  • User engagement metrics
  • Performance monitoring

Preference Cookies

  • Theme settings
  • Language preferences
  • Video player settings

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser settings: Configure your browser to block or alert you about cookies
  • Cookie preferences: Use our cookie preference center to customize your choices
  • Third-party tools: Use privacy-focused browser extensions
  • Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use some third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Analytics services
  • Video hosting services
  • Social media integrations
  • Payment processors

6. Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain for a set period
  • Authentication cookies: Typically expire after 30 days

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
